Fill in the boxes below to calculate a patient's AMA disability rating for hearing impairment

Calculations based on Guides to the Evaluation of Permanent Impairment 6th Edition ©2008 American Medical Association

Note that I have discovered what appear to be math errors in the binaural impairment table in the book listed above. Rather than rely on a table look-up, all binaural data provided on this page is calculated based on the formula provided in the text. As a result, however, binaural impairment may differ by 0.1% depending on your source. It is also possible that they used a non-standard rounding scheme in their calculations, but if so, this was undocumented.
